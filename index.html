Winston Chen

I graduated from the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Washington in 2022.

I am currently a research assistant at the Noble Research Lab. I work with William Stafford Noble and Yang Lu on false discovery rate (FDR)-controlled machine learning methods for protein homology prediction and biological feature (e.g. transcription factors) interaction detection.

My research interests are:

  1. Developing reliable ML methods for high-precision biomedical applications.
  2. Error-controlled biological hypothesis generation with ML model interpretation.
